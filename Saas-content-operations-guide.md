## 1. Introduction
SaaS companies rely on consistent, high-quality content to grow brand visibility, educate users, and support product adoption.  
Content operations ensure everything is planned, produced, reviewed, and published efficiently.

---

## 2. What Are Content Operations?
Content operations is the system behind content creation:
- Research  
- Planning  
- Production  
- Optimization  
- Publishing  
- Tracking  

---

## 3. SaaS Content Workflow
Typical workflow:
1. Keyword/topic research  
2. Outline creation  
3. Draft writing  
4. Internal review  
5. SEO optimization  
6. CMS upload  
7. Image requests  
8. Publish  
9. Performance tracking
