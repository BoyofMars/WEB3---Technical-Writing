### ‚ö† FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt)
- Respond calmly  
- Provide facts  
- Share official links  

### ‚ö† Scams
- Warn users early  
- Ban malicious accounts  
- Verify every link before sharing  

### ‚ö† Fake Airdrops
- Remind users: *"No team member will DM you first."*

## 7. Handling Scams & Security Threats
Moderators must understand common threats:

- Phishing links  
- Fake admins  
- Impersonation  
- Giveaway scams  
- Pump & dump invitations  

Actions to take:
1. Delete harmful messages  
2. Ban the user  
3. Notify the core team  
4. Post a community warning
